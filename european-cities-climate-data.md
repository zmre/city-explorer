## DATA SOURCES & METHODOLOGY

Climate data compiled from:
- World Meteorological Organization databases
- European Climate Assessment & Dataset (ECA&D)
- National meteorological services (AEMET, IPMA, etc.)
- 30-year climate normals (1991-2020)
- Population data from national statistical offices (2023-2024 estimates)

**Note on "sunny days":** Defined as days with less than 30% cloud cover or more than 6 hours of sunshine. Ranges provided where exact figures vary by source.
