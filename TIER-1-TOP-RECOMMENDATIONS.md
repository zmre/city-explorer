## SELECTION CRITERIA USED

### Climate Factors (60% weight):
1. **Sunshine hours** (2,500+ preferred)
2. **Temperature stability** (60-80°F ideal range)
3. **Low humidity** (Mediterranean/dry climates preferred)
4. **Minimal extreme weather** (no hurricanes, tornadoes, extreme heat/cold)
5. **Air quality** (low pollution)

### Livability Factors (40% weight):
1. **Multiple ranking appearances** (3+ major lists)
2. **Infrastructure quality**
3. **Cultural amenities**
4. **Healthcare access**
5. **Outdoor recreation opportunities**
6. **Expat/international friendliness**

### Population Range:
- Target: 50,000-1,000,000
- Exceptions made for: San Diego, Santa Barbara, Perth (climate excellence outweighs size)

## REGIONAL SUMMARIES

### EUROPE - SPAIN DOMINATES
**Top Picks:** Valencia, Málaga, Alicante
- Spain has 6 of top 15 European moderate-climate cities
- Mediterranean coast = sunshine + culture + affordability
- Language barrier exists but expat communities strong

### NORTH AMERICA - CALIFORNIA COASTAL EXCELLENCE
**Top Picks:** San Diego, Santa Barbara
- California coast has best weather in North America
- High cost of living major drawback
- Exceptional outdoor recreation access

### OCEANIA - CLEAN AIR CHAMPIONS
**Top Picks:** Adelaide, Perth, Nelson, Wellington
- Cleanest air globally
- Isolated locations (especially Perth)
- High quality of life, outdoor lifestyle
- English speaking

### AFRICA - UNEXPECTED GEM
**Top Pick:** Cape Town
- Only African city in top tier
- Stunning natural beauty + perfect climate
- Affordability + adventure appeal
- Safety considerations exist

### SOUTH AMERICA - ETERNAL SPRING HAVENS
**Top Picks:** Cuenca, Arequipa, Loja
- High-altitude eternal spring climates
- Very affordable
- Remote/developing infrastructure
- Growing expat communities

### ASIA - MOUNTAIN RETREAT CITIES
**Limited Options:** Dali, Da Lat, Pokhara, Baguio
- High-altitude locations for moderate temps
- Eternal spring climates
- Less developed than other regions
- Adventure/nature focused
