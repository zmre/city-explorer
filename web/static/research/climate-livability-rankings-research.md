- Sunshine hours from meteorological services
- Köppen climate classification
- Temperature and precipitation averages
- Humidity and pollution data where available

### Population Verification:
- City proper vs. metropolitan area distinctions noted
- Target range: 50,000-1,000,000
- Some exceptions made for world-class climate cities slightly outside range
