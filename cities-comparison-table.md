## REGIONAL ANALYSIS

### MEDITERRANEAN COASTAL (Best overall climate)
**Top picks:** Malaga, Alicante, Nice, Split, Heraklion
- Highest sunshine hours (270-320 days)
- Moderate humidity (58-74% range)
- Mild winters (5-10°C lows)
- Warm but not extreme summers (27-31°C)
- Most have mountain proximity

### ATLANTIC COASTAL (Cooler, more rain)
**Top picks:** Porto, Faro
- Fewer sunny days (220-300)
- Higher humidity (68-82%)
- Milder summers (25-29°C)
- Cool winters (5-9°C lows)

### BLACK SEA (Eastern Europe option)
**Top picks:** Varna, Burgas, Constanta
- Good sunshine (230-250 days)
- Higher humidity (66-82%)
- Moderate temperatures (26-28°C summer)
- Colder winters (-2 to 1°C)

### INLAND MEDITERRANEAN (Hot, dry summers)
**Top picks:** Granada, Murcia, Verona, Bologna
- Very high sunshine (205-315 days)
- Lower humidity in summer
- Hot summers (30-35°C)
- Colder winters (-1 to 5°C)
